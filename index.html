About TLEC

Based out of Ithaca, NY, we take a holistic approach by using a combination of science and technology to maximize health and athletic performance!

Mission Statement

We use the Treigning Lab template and work hand in hand with Coach Sam Calavitta in order to create and develop the most comprehensive and detailed performance and recovery programs in the world for not only elite athletes but all people. We strive for peak performance as well as optimal health and wellness.

Philosophy

We work to optimize health and wellness through a holistic approach. Accounting for psychological and physiological factors, we develop and guide an individual through a customized fully encompassing action plan.

Our Team

Frank Perrelli

Head Trainer. Treigning Lab Affiliate. Has been under the tutilage of Sam Calavitta as an athlete and/ or trainer since 2018. As an athlete, Frank was a High School State Champion Wrestler in New Jersey. From there we went on to become a Division 1 All- American at Cornell University, graduating in 2012. After college, he continued wrestling Freestyle domestically and internationally until 2021. He was a US Open Runner up and 7 time US Open All-American. In 2021 he transitioned to his current position as Head RTC at Spartan Combat RTC. Since beginning at Spartan he has coached athletes to 15 World Team berths, 4 UWW World Championship Gold Medals, 2 UWW World Championship Silver, and one UWW World Championship Bronze Medal. Outside of coaching wrestling, he develops his athlete's strength and conditioning, nutrition, and weight cut programs.
